INTRODUCTION

At Lentil Luxe Overseas LLP, we understand the responsibility that comes with working in the food industry. In a country like India, where agriculture is the backbone of our economy, we recognize the deep connection between our food and the land that nurtures it. We believe in more than just producing food – we aim to do it better, with care and consciousness for both the environment and the health of our consumers.

Our focus is on delivering high-quality, safe, and nutritious food, because we know that what we put on the table has the power to improve lives. We take pride in ensuring every step of the process reflects our commitment to excellence, from seed to plate. At Lentil Luxe, it’s not about producing more – it’s about producing better. After all, true quality can only be achieved when you take the utmost care at every stage of the journey.

Wholesome Pulses – Nature’s Protein Powerhouse

Red Lentils (Masoor Dal)

Red Lentils are packed with protein and vitamins, ideal for soups and curries. Free from Gluten, GMOs, and allergens.

Chickpeas (White Chana)

White Chickpeas are high in protein and fiber. Perfect for salads and curries. Free from Gluten, GMOs, and allergens.

Red Kidney Bean (Rajma)

High in protein and fiber, Red Kidney Beans are perfect for curries and stews. Free from Gluten, GMOs, and allergens.

Split Chickpea Lentils (Chana Dal)

Chana Dal, or split chickpeas, offers a nutty flavor and is ideal for curries, soups, and stews. It's a nutritious, gluten-free addition to your meals
