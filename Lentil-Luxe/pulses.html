PULSES

Our premium pulses like Black Lentils, Red Lentils, Toor Dal, and Red kidney bean are sourced from trusted local farms. Rich in protein and fiber, they are perfect for nutritious meals. Free from Gluten, GMOs, and allergens, they offer superior taste and quality, making them a healthy kitchen essential.

Red Lentils (Masoor Dal)
