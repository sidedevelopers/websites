Who We Are

Bringing Authentic Flavours to Every Indian Kitchen Since 1992

Bregano is more than just a brand—it’s a tradition of excellence in food manufacturing. Established by Dwarika Group in 1992, our journey began with a small Atta Chakki in Rudrapur, Uttarakhand. Today, we bring premium-quality staples, pasta, condiments, and nutritious food products to homes across India, ensuring taste, health, and convenience in every meal.

Guided by the vision of Mr. Balram Aggrawal, Bregano upholds the highest standards of quality, innovation, and trust. Our state-of-the-art, fully automated 7-acre facility follows FSSAI, GMP, and GHP food safety standards, ensuring that every product meets the expectations of modern households.

From 100% semolina pasta (No Maida) to pure, nutrient-rich grains, our commitment is to delivering authenticity and wellness in every bite. Whether it’s a quick snack or a hearty meal, Bregano is your trusted partner in the kitchen.
