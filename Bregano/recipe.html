Seviyan (Vermicelli)
Upma Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Bregano Vermicelli (made from 100% suji/semolina)
  • 1.5 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tbsp peanuts
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (rai)
  • 1 dry red chilli
  • A pinch of hing (asafoetida)
  • 5–6 curry leaves
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chilli paste
  • 1.5 cups mixed vegetables (any available – e.g., carrots, capsicum, beans, peas)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1.5 cups water

Recipe:

  • Dry Roast Vermicelli:
    Heat 1 tsp ghee in a pan. Add 1 cup Bregano vermicelli and roast on low flame until it turns light golden brown. Remove and keep aside.
  • Prepare Tempering:
    In the same pan, add more ghee (if needed). Add chana dal, urad dal, peanuts, mustard seeds, dry red chilli, hing, and curry leaves. Sauté until aromatic.
  • Sauté Onions & Aromatics:
    Add chopped onion and ginger-green chilli paste. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add Veggies & Spices:
    Add all your chopped vegetables (use whatever is available at home). Then add:
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chilli powder
  • Pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste:
    Mix well.
  • Add Water & Simmer:
    Pour in 1.5 cups water. Mix and bring to a boil.
  • Cook Vermicelli:
    Add the roasted vermicelli. Mix gently. Cover and cook on low flame for 10–12 minutes, or until water is absorbed and vermicelli is cooked through.
  • Fluff & Serve:
    Once done, fluff with a fork. The vermicelli will be soft, non-sticky, and perfectly cooked.
  • ✅Serving Tip:
    Garnish with chopped coriander or a squeeze of lemon for added freshness. Serve hot!
