About Us

Rooted in Legacy
Driven by Quality

Founded in 1992 in Rudrapur, Uttarakhand, Dwarika Foods Private Limited began its journey as a humble Atta Chakki, driven by a deep-rooted belief in delivering quality and honesty in every grain. Over the years, this belief evolved into Bregano—a trusted FMCG food brand catering to modern Indian households and global markets alike.
Today, Bregano stands for purity, taste, and innovation. With a presence in 10+ Indian states and partnerships with retail giants like Patanjali, Vishal Mega Mart, and Walmart, we continue to uphold our commitment to crafting food that not only nourishes but also connects families through the flavours of tradition.

Our Mission

To craft honest, healthy, and flavourful food products that meet everyday needs while exceeding expectations—delivering trust, nutrition, and joy to every kitchen we reach.

Our Mission

To become the most trusted Indian food brand in global markets, by consistently blending authenticity with innovation and offering food solutions that are as nourishing as they are delicious.

We Are

Reliable Manufacturer for Quality FMCG Foods

At Dwarika Foods, manufacturing is more than a process—it's a promise. Operating from a state-of-the-art 7-acre facility in Rudrapur, we proudly produce over 50 metric tonnes of food products daily using globally certified practices (FSSAI, GMP, GHP).
From 100% semolina pasta made on advanced Italian Pavan machinery to essential Indian staples and condiments, every product is developed through a fully automated, hygienic, and quality-controlled environment. Our ability to scale with precision, customise packaging, and meet bulk export requirements positions us not just as a manufacturer, but as a reliable partner for brands across the world.
